I'm living my creatively nerdy dream while studying multimedia design.

Emilie Kure

This is what I can do

Up until this point of my studies, I have worked a little bit with Adobe Creative Cloud - so far just Photoshop and XD, where I created a prototype for my portfolio website, but I also deviated from the curriculum a bit, and played around in Illustrator, where I created my logo.

I have also done a lot of coding with HTML5 and CSS3, skills I have used to create this first version of my personal portfolio website. I have a rather clear vision of the style I want the site to have, and where I want to go design-wise, and I am beyond excited for what is to come.

This is what I will learn to do

Although I have already tested the waters in Illustrator, I will at a later point receive proper instructions in how to use the program, along with other CC programs, such as Premiere Pro and InDesign.

Additionally, I plan on developing my skills in Photoshop much further, and I will also learn JavaScript to further advance my portfolio website, as well as future coding projects.

This is what I want do

When I finish my AP degree in Multimedia Design, I plan on applying to the top-up bachelor's degree in Web Development.

My goal is to work freelance, and be able to use everything I have learnt and everything I still have yet to learn, in my daily work.
