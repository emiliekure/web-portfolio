First edit in Photoshop

1st semester

Scope

During our introductory week, we went on an excursion to Copenhagen, where the objective was to take pictures of bridges of different types. Back at school we then had to choose one image picturing a bridge, and edit the following things in Photoshop:

  • Remove an element so it blends in with the rest
  • Add an element so it blends in with the rest
  • Adjust light or add a filter
  • Crop and (if we wanted to) straighten
  • Resize to 4:3 format and file size max 1 megapixels

Ideas and thoughts

My approach to this assignment was very simple.

My first idea was to replace the sky with a sunset sky, because the sunset would give room to adjust the colors, to match the sky.

Then I spent a good amount of time removing the graffiti on the side of the bridge with auto-fill in selections.

Lastly I cropped the photo and made sure the format fitted 4:3, so that less of the water was visible.
