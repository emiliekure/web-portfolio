Portfolio prototype in XD

1st semester

Scope

For this assignment, we were tasked with developing af prototype of our pending portfolio website, in Adobe XD, or a similar program.

Additionally, we had to create a short document, where we described and showcased our prototype.

Ideas and thoughts

I wanted to keep my prototype simple, with corresponding shapes and colors, so I used green, grey and circles throughout, as well as just one font family.

I realised when I showcased my prototype on Zoom to the class, that my font size was way too big, because I didn't think about or check how it would look in practice.
